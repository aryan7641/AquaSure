Connecting Water Suppliers Across The Country

In many areas, the shortage of water or the supply of un-purified water poses a significant health risk to people. That's why we are bringing the offline drinking water supplier model online to make ordering and managing delivery simple and efficient.

The Problems
with Offline Transactions

Offline transactions are hard to manage, and distributors often face delayed responses and services. Communicating through offline mediums is time-consuming, and the authenticity of distributors' services cannot be checked. But with our online platform, we offer a solution to these problems.

The Novelty of Our Online Platform

Our online medium is easy and convenient, with payment and bills managed easily. People can give reviews and ratings based on the services they receive, and the reach of suppliers will increase. Anyone can order by themselves, without the hassle of contacting a distributor.
